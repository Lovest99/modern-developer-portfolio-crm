TESTIMONIALS BACKGROUND IMAGE

Please place your testimonials background image in this directory with the filename:
testimonials-bg.jpg

The image should be:
- High resolution (at least 1920x1080px)
- Subtle enough to not distract from the testimonial content
- Preferably with neutral colors that work well with both light and dark themes

Good options include:
- Abstract patterns
- Blurred professional settings
- Subtle textures
- Gradient backgrounds

Until you add your own image, the component will look for the image at:
/images/backgrounds/testimonials-bg.jpg

You can download free high-quality background images from sites like:
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Freepik (https://freepik.com)
